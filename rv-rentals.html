Frequently asked questions

Do I have to drive the RV?

No. We deliver, set up, and pick it up — you never tow or drive it. The keys stay with us.

Where do you deliver?

Any campground within about 50 miles of Monroe, TN. Farther out? Contact us for a custom quote ($4/mile beyond 50).

Do I need RV insurance?

No. Our vehicles are fully insured, and since you're not driving, there's nothing for you to cover.

How do I book the campsite?

You reserve your own campsite at the campground of your choice and we deliver to it. On-farm stays are booked right here with us.

What deposits are there?

A 50% deposit reserves your dates. The remaining balance is due 24 hours before check-in. At check-in we collect a refundable $500 incidental (damage) deposit, returned after inspection.

Are pets allowed?

Yes — pets are welcome.

How many does it sleep?

It varies by unit — just ask when you book and we'll match you to the right RV.
